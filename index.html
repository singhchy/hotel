Our Story

A Legacy of
Timeless Hospitality

Founded in 1923 within a meticulously restored 19th-century Parisian manor, Auberge Céleste has welcomed discerning travelers for over a century. What began as a family passion project has evolved into one of Europe's most celebrated luxury boutique hotels.

Our philosophy rests on three pillars: impeccable service, design authenticity, and environmental stewardship. Every chandelier, every thread of linen, every greeting at the door is an expression of our unwavering commitment to the art of genuine hospitality.

✓ Sustainable Luxury — carbon-neutral operations since 2020
✓ Heritage Preservation — supporting local artisans and culture
✓ Community Engagement — local sourcing and employment
